## The challenge

Zidney's learning platform had grown feature by feature until the daily
experience felt like work *about* studying rather than studying itself.
Students bounced between disconnected screens, and the most common action —
"what do I do next?" — took the most clicks.

## What I did

I ran a discovery sprint with students and teachers, mapped the real daily
journey, and rebuilt the experience around a single focused workspace.
