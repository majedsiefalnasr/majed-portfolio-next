## The challenge

Pulse shipped every chart a customer ever asked for. The result was a
dashboard nobody could read: twelve widgets per screen and a support team
teaching people where to look.

## What I did

I interviewed the support team first — they knew every question customers
actually asked. Each screen was rebuilt around one question with one primary
answer, and everything else moved a click away.

## The outcome

Time-to-insight in usability tests dropped 38%, and "where do I find" support
tickets fell by half.
