## The challenge

Bloom's retention curve looked like every other wellness app: a motivated
first week, then guilt-driven streak mechanics that pushed people away the
moment they missed a day.

## What I did

We replaced streaks with a five-minute daily check-in that always ends in a
small win, and rewrote every notification from a nag into an invitation.

## The outcome

Thirty-day retention more than doubled, and the App Store reviews stopped
mentioning the word "guilt".
