## Engagement is not success

Time-on-page goes up when users are lost. Clicks go up when nothing is
findable. A dashboard full of green can describe a product that's quietly
failing its users.

## Measure completion, not activity

Pick the one task each screen exists for and measure how often people finish
it — and how often they come back unprompted. Those two numbers are hard to
fool.
