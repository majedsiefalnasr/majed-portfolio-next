## The redesign reflex

When metrics dip, the instinct is to repaint the interface. But most products
don't have a visual problem — they have a comprehension problem, and a fresh
coat of paint relocates it instead of fixing it.

## Where the answers already live

Your support inbox is a free, continuously updated usability study. Cluster
one month of tickets by the screen they mention and you'll know exactly where
the product loses people — no workshop required.
