## The challenge

Aurora's booking flow was built for solo travelers, but most of their bookings
came from small groups planning together. The flow collapsed the moment a
second person got involved: links were shared over chat apps, choices got
lost, and carts expired before anyone agreed.

## What I did

I mapped how groups actually decide — shortlists, vetoes, and a single payer —
and redesigned the flow around a shared shortlist instead of a personal cart.

## The outcome

Group bookings stopped leaking into chat apps, and completed bookings rose
41% in the first quarter after launch.
